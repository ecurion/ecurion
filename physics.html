Visualising the Hydrogen Atom

Radial part :

$$R_{n,l}(r) = C_1(n,l) \cdot e^{-r/na_0} \cdot (r/na_0)^l \cdot L_{n-l-1}^{2l+1}(r/2na_0)$$

where $a_0$ is the Bohr radius and $L_{n-l-1}^{2l+1}$ are the associated Laguerre polynomials given by :

$$L^{p}_{q} = \frac{e^{x}x^{-p}}{q!}\frac{d}{dx}^q\left(e^{x}x^{-p}\right)$$

Angular part :

$$Y_{l,m}(\theta,\phi) = C_2(l,m)\cdot P_{l,m}(\cos\theta) \cdot e^{im\phi}$$

def R_nl(r,n,l):
    pdf = (np.exp(-r/n)*(2*r/n)**l*F.assoc_laguerre(r,n-l-1,k=2*l+1))**2
    dos = 4*np.pi*r**2
    return pdf*dos

def T_lm(theta,l,m):
    pdf = F.lpmv(m,l,np.cos(theta))**2
    dos = 2*np.pi*np.sin(theta)
    return pdf*dos
